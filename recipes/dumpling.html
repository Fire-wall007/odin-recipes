Dumpling Recipe

History of Dumpling

Dumplings are traditionally eaten during the Lunar New Years Celebration. It's formed in the shape of a "Goden Nugget" and sybolizes as good fortune.

Dumpling also come in many flavors such as pork and chives, shrimp with mushrooms, and beef with cabages.

Ingredients

  • Premade Dumpling Skin
  • Ground Pork (1 lb)
  • Chinese Chives (.5 lb)
  • Green Onion (2)
  • Soy Sauce (2 tbsp)
  • Salt (1 tbsp)
  • White Pepper (.5 tsp)
  • Oyster Sauce (2 tbsp)

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Let it sit for 30 min - 1 hr
  3. Fill dumpling skin with fillings
  4. Steam dumplings for 10 mins
  5. ENJOY!
